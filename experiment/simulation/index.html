Half wave/Full wave Rectifier

Objective:

  1.  To differentiate between input and output waveforms in Half wave/full wave rectifier
  2.  To calculate the Ripple Factor

Apparatus Used:

Diodes, Step down Transformer, Resistor, DSO

DSO

Diode

Resistor

Transformer




N1:primary turns

N2:secondary turns

Resistor: Ω

Frequency: 50Hz





Instructions

  1. Select half or full wave rectifier and type of transformer from the menu.
  2. Set the primary turns,secondary turns and resistance value and press the "Connect DSO" button to connect the channels of dso to the rectifier circuit.
  3. Click the "Get input waveform" button and analyse the input waveform with and without the diode.
  4. Click the "Get output waveform" button and analyse the rectified output waveform.
  5. Click on "Show Result" button to check the calculations.
  6. Calculate the value of Vm ,V rms, V dc and ripple factor, γ=sqrt((Vrms/Vdc)^2-1).
  7. Click on the "Reset" button to restart the experiment.

CALCULATIONS: Full Wave Rectifier

  • From N 1 * V 2 = N 2 * V 1
  • N 1= Number of turns in Primary transformer , N 2= Number of turns in Secondary transformer
  • V 1= Primary Voltage , V 2 = Secondary Voltage
  • Here, V 2 = V m,V rms= V m/ √2
  • V dc= (2 * V m)/ π
  • Ripple factor of Full wave Rectifier is, γ = √(V rms/ V dc) 2 - 1
  • Theoretical Value of Ripple Factor ≈0.48
